About Regence Group

A Regional Platform Built on Trust, Expertise, and Long-Term Thinking

Our Story

Regence Group was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge world-class watch brands with the enormous potential of Southeast Asia and the Indian Ocean Region.

We recognised that the region's watch markets — diverse, dynamic, and fast-evolving — required more than a traditional distributor. They required a partner with local knowledge, regional infrastructure, and the strategic mindset to build brands for the long term.

Today, Regence Group represents a portfolio of distinguished Swiss and European timepiece brands, serving a network of authorised retail partners across the region with professionalism, precision, and genuine commercial partnership.

Our Approach

We believe that great distribution is invisible. When done right, the consumer sees only the brand — beautifully presented, consistently positioned, and available through the right channels.

Behind that experience, Regence Group provides the infrastructure, relationships, and market expertise that make it possible.

We are selective in the brands we represent and intentional in the markets we enter. Our measure of success is the sustained, long-term growth of every brand entrusted to us.

Why Partner With Regence

For brand principals seeking regional representation, Regence Group offers:

  • Exclusive, accountable distribution with full brand alignment
  • An established retail network across Southeast Asia and the Indian Ocean Region
  • Dedicated market development, including retailer training and co-marketing
  • Transparent reporting and joint business planning
  • A team that speaks both the language of luxury and the realities of regional retail
